Top Objections

"You don't have SOC 2."
Response: "We're on that roadmap and progressing. Today we have SSO, contractual no-training commitment, $1M IP indemnification, and a security posture that's passed procurement at reference customers. The real question is: do you want SOC 2 on a video-only tool or a stock library, or do you want the full AI creative platform your team will love — with compliance certifications coming soon?"

Data point: Only 3 competitors have SOC 2 Type II (Adobe at corporate level, Runway, LTX Studio). The others are aligned, Type I, or nonexistent.
"Adobe/Canva already does this."
Response: "Adobe Firefly is one model with conservative outputs, bundled inside Creative Cloud for $60-90/seat. Canva is a template tool with limited AI generation. OpenArt has 100+ models, custom brand training, consistent characters, and the fastest-shipping engineering team in the industry — at $59/seat standalone. We're not competing with Adobe's editing suite. We're replacing the part of your creative workflow that AI does better."

Data point: OpenArt has 100+ models; Firefly has one family. OpenArt ships every 2-3 weeks; Adobe ships quarterly.
"Why not just use Midjourney?"
Response: "Midjourney has no enterprise offering — no SSO, no admin controls, no team management, no IP indemnification, no floating licenses. It's a consumer tool in a Discord bot. For individual artists it's fine. For a team of 10-100 people who need governance, it's not an option."

Data point: Midjourney has zero enterprise features. OpenArt has SSO, admin controls, pooled credits, floating licenses, $1M IP indemnification.
"FreePik/Higgsfield has IP indemnification too."
For FreePik: "FreePik matches our $1M IP indemnification. The difference is what you're protecting. FreePik is a stock library with some AI; OpenArt is purpose-built for AI generation with 100+ models and custom training. Same legal protection, 10x the creative capability."

For Higgsfield: "Higgsfield has zero documented IP indemnification. That's often a dealbreaker in procurement."
"Your competitor has more users / higher valuation."
Response: "User counts and valuations don't tell you about product quality or enterprise readiness. Here's what matters: 230,000 people pay for OpenArt. Our revenue grew 7x in one year. Our 20-person team ships a major release every 2-3 weeks. We're the most capital-efficient, fastest-moving team in the category. That's the team and product you're partnering with."
